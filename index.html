Academia



I am currently studying for my Master's degree in Computer Science with a minor in Computational Neurosciences at the Swiss Federal Institude of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL), the top 12th univesity in the world. I am currently working at the Distributed Computing Laboratory on Machine Learning problems, both on theory and on experimental sections. After finishing my Master's degree I would like to pursue an academic career and see a PhD in Computer Science as a next step.
Last year I have created a seamless augmented reality library working in the CHILI lab. The goal was to first get rid of conventinoal black-and-white markers which spoil the scene visually, and seconly, make it run on Android tablets with decent latency, both goals were achieved.
In 2017 I have graduated from the Moscow Institude of Physics and Technology, the leading technical university in Russia with a Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics.
As part of my BSc thesis, I have worked on the paper on optimization and on a library CAQM for the paper. I participate in software development and scientific competitions and present my findings at conferences.

Industry



Moreover, I have obtained industrial experience both working for small startups and creating my own. I have worked in the field of High-Frequency Trading in 2015-2016, developing in C++ an application from initial statement to production and supporting and I was responsible for the performance of the code which gave me a lot of experience in low-level networking and parallelism. I regularly performed benchmarks and tweaked the internals of the algorithm to make it run faster as this was the key property which made the team profit.
In addition, in 2015 I have created a startup called EscapeControl which provides real-life escape rooms with a software framework. It allows to set up all of the escape room logic after the hardware is already installed via a simple and intuitive web-interface, instead of opening up the walls and reflashing microcontrollers, thus reducing the construction time by weeks. The interface also allows to see the source of the problem quickly which makes the process of repairs much faster, and thus saves money and time. It is currently running in different cities across the globe. My roles in this project are various, starting from managing the team and writing backend code all the way to customer support and sales.
